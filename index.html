常見問題 / FAQ

進度會不見嗎？ Will progress be lost?
學習進度只存在裝置上，可用 App 內的「匯出 / 匯入」備份。刪除 App 會一併清除進度。
Progress is stored only on your device. Use in-app Export / Import to back it up. Deleting the app removes it.

付費版包含什麼？ What does the paid version include?
Lumi 注音星球 Pro 一次購買、永久使用，所有關卡與練習內容全部解鎖，無內購、無廣告。
Lumi Bopomofo Pro is a one-time purchase with all levels and practice content unlocked — no in-app purchases, no ads.

支援哪些裝置？ Supported devices?
支援 iPhone 與 iPad。Works on iPhone and iPad.

隱私政策 / Privacy Policy

生效日期 Effective date: 2026-05-25

  • 我們不蒐集任何個人資料。We do not collect any personal data.
  • 無帳號、無註冊、無登入。No accounts, no sign-up, no login.
  • 無廣告、無第三方分析或追蹤。No ads, no third-party analytics or tracking.
  • 所有資料都不會離開你的裝置。No data ever leaves your device.

App 僅在裝置本機儲存孩子的學習進度（已解鎖的字、星星、聽過的聲調、設定）。此資料不會傳送給我們或任何人，刪除 App 即清除。
The app stores your child's learning progress only on the device. It is never transmitted to us or anyone, and is removed when you delete the app.

選用的「匯出 / 匯入」功能會將進度檔存到你自己的裝置／檔案 App，由你選擇位置，我們不會收到或保存該檔。
The optional Export / Import saves the progress file to your own device/Files app, chosen by you. We never receive or store it.

兒童隱私 Children's privacy：本 App 為兒童（約 4–7 歲）設計，因完全不蒐集個人資訊，符合 COPPA 與 GDPR-K，無行為廣告、無第三方資料分享。
Designed for children (~4–7). Because it collects no personal info, it complies with COPPA and GDPR-K; no behavioral ads, no third-party data sharing.

權限 Permissions：不要求相機、麥克風、定位、聯絡人、照片或通知等敏感權限；音訊與語音皆於裝置本機運作。
No sensitive permissions (camera, mic, location, contacts, photos, notifications). Audio and text-to-speech run entirely on device.
