FAQ

Frequently asked questions 常見問題

Everything about progress, editions, purchases and privacy.

Will my child's progress be lost?學習進度會不會不見？+
Progress is stored only on your device. Use the in-app Export / Import to back it up; deleting the app removes it. 進度只儲存在你的裝置上。可用 App 內的「匯出 / 匯入」功能備份；刪除 App 會一併清除進度。
What's the difference between the free and Pro versions?免費版和 Pro 版有什麼不同？+
"Lumi Bopomofo" (free) lets you try part of the content for free, with an optional one-time "full unlock" in-app purchase. "Lumi Bopomofo Pro" is a single purchase with everything unlocked and no in-app purchases. Neither version contains ads. 免費版可免費體驗部分內容，提供一次性「完整解鎖」內購；Pro 版一次購買、全部解鎖、無任何內購。兩版皆無廣告。
How do I restore a purchase on a new device?換新裝置後如何回復購買？+
Sign in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase and tap "Restore Purchases" in the app's Parents section. Apple re-validates your purchase at no extra cost. 用原本購買的 Apple ID 登入，於 App 的「家長」頁點「回復購買」，Apple 會免費重新驗證你的購買。
Is in-app purchase payment safe? How do refunds work?內購付款安全嗎？如何退款？+
All purchases are handled by the Apple App Store; we never receive or store your payment information or Apple ID. For refunds, use Apple's official refund process at reportaproblem.apple.com. 所有購買皆由 Apple App Store 處理，我們不會收到或儲存付款資訊或 Apple ID。退款請至 reportaproblem.apple.com 透過 Apple 官方流程辦理。
Which ages and devices are supported? Does it work offline?適合幾歲？支援哪些裝置？可離線嗎？+
Designed for children around ages 4–7, on both iPhone and iPad. The app works fully offline — no internet connection is required to play. 專為約 4–7 歲設計，支援 iPhone 與 iPad，且完全可離線遊玩，不需要網路連線。
Does the app collect any personal data about my child?App 會蒐集孩子的個人資料嗎？+
No. The app collects no personal data, and has no accounts, ads or third-party tracking — everything stays on the device. See the full privacy policy below. 不會。App 不蒐集任何個人資料，無帳號、廣告或第三方追蹤，所有資料都留在裝置上。詳見下方隱私政策。

01 What we store

The app stores your child's learning progress (unlocked characters, stars, tones heard, and settings) only on the device. It is never transmitted to us or any third party, and is removed when you delete the app. App 僅在裝置本機儲存孩子的學習進度（已解鎖的字、星星、聽過的聲調與設定）。此資料不會傳送給我們或任何第三方，並在你刪除 App 時一併移除。

02 Backup & Restore

The optional Export / Import feature saves the progress file to your own device / Files app, in a location you choose. We never receive or store this file. 選用的「匯出 / 匯入」功能會將進度檔存到你自己的裝置／檔案 App，位置由你決定，我們不會收到或保存該檔。

03 Purchases

The free edition offers an optional one-time "full unlock" in-app purchase; all transactions are handled by the Apple App Store. We never receive or store your payment information, card number, or Apple ID. The Pro edition contains no in-app purchases. 免費版提供選用的一次性「完整解鎖」內購，所有交易皆由 Apple App Store 處理。我們不會收到或儲存你的付款資訊、信用卡號或 Apple ID。Pro 版本身不含任何內購。

04 Children's privacy

The app is designed for children (around ages 4–7). Because it collects no personal information at all, it complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU GDPR-K. There is no behavioral advertising and no third-party data sharing. 本 App 為兒童（約 4–7 歲）設計。因完全不蒐集個人資訊，符合美國 COPPA 與歐盟 GDPR-K，沒有行為廣告，也沒有任何第三方資料分享。

05 Permissions

The app requests no sensitive permissions (no camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or notifications). Audio playback and text-to-speech run entirely on the device. App 不要求相機、麥克風、定位、聯絡人、照片或通知等任何敏感權限；音訊播放與語音合成皆於裝置本機運作。

06 Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we will update this page and the effective date above. Questions are always welcome at hourstag.app@gmail.com. 若本政策有任何變動，我們會更新本頁面與上方的生效日期。如有疑問，歡迎來信 hourstag.app@gmail.com。
