常見問題 FAQ

關於進度、版本差異與購買的常見疑問。

學習進度會不會不見？+
進度只儲存在你的裝置上。可使用 App 內的「匯出 / 匯入」功能備份；刪除 App 會一併清除進度。 Progress is stored only on your device. Use the in-app Export / Import to back it up. Deleting the app removes it.
免費版和 Pro 版有什麼不同？+
「Lumi 注音星球」（免費版）可免費體驗部分內容，並提供一次性的「完整解鎖」內購來開啟全部關卡；「Lumi 注音星球 Pro」則是一次購買、永久使用，內容全部解鎖、沒有任何內購。兩個版本都沒有廣告。 "Lumi Bopomofo" (free) lets you try part of the content for free with an optional one-time "full unlock" purchase; "Lumi Bopomofo Pro" is a single purchase with everything unlocked and no in-app purchases. Neither version contains ads.
內購的付款資料安全嗎？如何退款？+
所有購買皆由 Apple App Store 處理，我們不會收到或儲存你的付款資訊或 Apple ID。如需退款，請透過 Apple 的官方退款流程辦理。 All purchases are handled by the Apple App Store; we never receive or store your payment information or Apple ID. For refunds, please use Apple's official refund process.
適合幾歲？支援哪些裝置？+
專為約 4–7 歲的學齡前與低年級兒童設計，同時支援 iPhone 與 iPad。 Designed for children around ages 4–7, and supports both iPhone and iPad.
App 會不會蒐集孩子的個人資料？+
不會。App 不蒐集任何個人資料，沒有帳號、廣告或第三方追蹤，所有資料都留在裝置上。詳見下方隱私政策。 No. The app collects no personal data, and has no accounts, ads or third-party tracking — everything stays on the device. See the privacy policy below.

01 我們儲存什麼 What we store

App 僅在裝置本機儲存孩子的學習進度（已解鎖的字、星星、聽過的聲調與設定）。此資料不會傳送給我們或任何第三方，並在你刪除 App 時一併移除。 The app stores your child's learning progress (unlocked characters, stars, tones heard, settings) only on the device. It is never transmitted to us or any third party, and is removed when you delete the app.

02 備份／還原 Backup & Restore

選用的「匯出 / 匯入」功能會將進度檔存到你自己的裝置／檔案 App，位置由你決定，我們不會收到或保存該檔。 The optional Export / Import saves the progress file to your own device / Files app, chosen by you. We never receive or store it.

03 購買 Purchases

免費版提供選用的一次性「完整解鎖」內購；所有交易皆由 Apple App Store 處理。我們不會收到或儲存你的付款資訊、信用卡號或 Apple ID。Pro 版本身不含任何內購。 The free edition offers an optional one-time "full unlock" in-app purchase; all transactions are handled by the Apple App Store. We never receive or store your payment information, card number, or Apple ID. The Pro edition contains no in-app purchases.

04 兒童隱私 Children's privacy

本 App 為兒童（約 4–7 歲）設計。因完全不蒐集個人資訊，符合美國《兒童線上隱私保護法》(COPPA) 與歐盟 GDPR-K，沒有行為廣告，也沒有任何第三方資料分享。 Designed for children (~4–7). As it collects no personal information, it complies with the U.S. COPPA and EU GDPR-K — no behavioral advertising and no third-party data sharing.

05 權限 Permissions

App 不要求相機、麥克風、定位、聯絡人、照片或通知等任何敏感權限；音訊播放與語音合成皆於裝置本機運作。 The app requests no sensitive permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, notifications). Audio playback and text-to-speech run entirely on device.

06 政策變更 Changes

若本政策有任何變動，我們會更新本頁面與上方的生效日期。如有疑問，歡迎來信 hourstag.app@gmail.com。 If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date above. Questions? Email hourstag.app@gmail.com.
